# Full Audit Report: *Teaching Cø / N\**

**Edition:** 2.1

**Audit date:** July 18, 2026

**Status:** **Pass for controlled graduate-level teaching distribution**

**Residual status:** No known blocking defect. Empirical theory validation, specialist accessibility testing, and measured classroom outcome validation remain open by design.

## 1. Executive assessment

The manual is now a coherent, accessible graduate-level introduction to the seven-source Cø / N\* research program. It places the proposal among major theories of consciousness, distinguishes the source papers from later teaching conventions, develops each component and its evidential role, and treats differentiation and validation as adversarial research programs rather than accomplished facts.

This audit examined the theory synthesis, formal rules, comparative framing, fourteen-session pedagogy, collaborative work, assessment, worksheets, audience controls, build process, DOCX files, master PDFs, and all individually distributable reveal PDFs. It identified and corrected weaknesses in six areas:

1. **Formal completeness:** some keyed conclusions lacked a required interval, threshold, or minimality condition.
2. **Inferential discipline:** profile results, bearer results, boundary statuses, transport claims, and empirical validation needed sharper separation.
3. **Exercise alignment:** several staged exercises disclosed evidence at the wrong time or requested conclusions not supported by the supplied packet.
4. **Worksheet usability:** two forms lacked writable response columns, one worksheet sequence conflicted with the staged exercise, and forms needed reliable dedicated-page placement.
5. **Audience safety and reproducibility:** individual reveals lacked independent accessibility guarantees, the audience inventory was not frozen exactly, corpus provenance was incomplete, and concurrent builds could collide.
6. **Document quality:** seven live contents links were broken, five sparse continuation pages remained, and a dense Session 6 interval table clipped values despite passing structural width checks.

All release-blocking defects found in those areas were corrected and the complete teaching package was rebuilt. The final result passes the source, audience, navigation, worksheet, table, font, metadata, pagination, rendering, and hash checks described below.

The verdict is deliberately limited. This audit supports the manual's **coherence, teachability, and artifact integrity**. It does not establish that Cø / N\* is true or empirically confirmed.

Four kinds of warrant were kept separate throughout:

- **source coherence:** the manual's rules fit together;
- **pedagogical alignment:** the materials elicit and assess the intended reasoning;
- **artifact integrity:** the delivered files match the source and pass mechanical and visual checks; and
- **theory validation:** independent evidence supports the truth or empirical adequacy of Cø / N\*.

This report establishes the first three only.

## 3. Findings and implemented corrections

### A. Corpus integration and theory placement

The manual now gives each source a distinct instructional role:

| Corpus contribution | Place in the manual |
|---|---|
| Core Cø / N\* formulation | Target, biconditional profile, components, thresholds, and base research logic |
| Consciousness without report | Causal availability, route independence, and safeguards against report substitution |
| Where is the conscious subject? | Candidate discovery, inclusion-minimal bearers, plurality, mapping, and boundary stability |
| Indeterminacy as scientific result | Positive, negative, indeterminate, and unlicensed outcomes with abstention rules |
| Ablating N\* | Criterion, mechanism, measurement, profile, grouped, and bearer-level ablations |
| From phenomenal presence to phenomenal character | Independently tested bridge from presence to relational character |
| Consciousness in the schematic | Public reconstructability, fault localization, and blind-reader legibility |

The comparison framework locates Cø / N\* relative to IIT, GNWT, recurrent processing approaches, higher-order approaches, predictive-processing families, biological theories, and eliminativist or illusionist positions. The manual now requires a frozen rival version, matched explanandum, matched unit of analysis, and matched empirical target before comparison. This prevents easy victories created by comparing minimal presence in one theory with access, report, richness, or metaphysical identity in another.

The differentiating claim is presented cautiously: Cø / N\* combines viability, content formation, causal availability, recurrent stabilization, and boundary identification in a conjunctive research profile. No single component is claimed as unique, and shared vocabulary is not treated as shared theory.

### B. Unit of analysis and inferential status

The core profile $V\land N_1\land N_2\land N_3$ could otherwise be read as attributing consciousness to every all-pass container. The manual now distinguishes:

- causally admissible test referents;
- ontic component profiles;
- estimated component profiles;
- the inclusion-minimal positive-bearer set; and
- a resolved-bearer license.

Component tests can therefore return negative or single-failure evidence without presupposing a positive bearer. Final subject attribution requires recomputation over the frozen candidate lattice. The text also separates same-specification multiplicity from mapped cross-specification nesting, and it does not treat content-level co-occurrence as proof of co-consciousness or diachronic personal identity.

The four evidential statuses are now operationalized consistently:

- **positive:** every required bound and license clears its frozen rule;
- **negative:** a licensed, direction-appropriate interval establishes decisive failure;
- **indeterminate:** valid evidence does not resolve the frozen rule; and
- **unlicensed:** candidate identity, validity, selectivity, independence, transport, or another prerequisite fails.

Failure to establish a pass is not converted into ontic absence. Hatted estimates are separated from unhatted properties. Equality, interval straddling, surrogate conflict, direction-specific negative rules, and equivalence margins are explicit.

### C. Boundary identification

The boundary procedure now requires candidate discovery before inspection of consciousness-linked outcomes, frozen candidate and mapping rules, held-out component evidence, inclusion-minimality, separability, replacement tests, and stability checks. It reports unique, multiple, absent, or indeterminate bearers only at a fixed specification, with nested results reserved for mapped grains or horizons.

The audit added or clarified:

- intervention positivity and consistency assumptions;
- causal-model adequacy;
- matched internal and external intervention budgets;
- candidate-size or dimension normalization;
- measurement and regime stability;
- separate candidate reliability $R_C$ and bearer-output stability $R_B$; and
- distinctions among constituent support, interfaces, enabling support, and bearer identity.

Problem Set 2B now states that the multiple candidates have no qualifying proper subsystems, so the answer key's plurality conclusion actually follows from inclusion-minimality.

### D. Component presentation and source fidelity

The $N_2$ account now separates causal availability from activation, diffusion, task access, report, executive control, node count, and fixed anatomy. It presents the historical scalar source formulation before the conservative interval-based teaching convention. The convention $K_A<2\Rightarrow H_A=0$ removes an undefined entropy edge case.

The $N_3$ treatment distinguishes bounded recurrent stabilization from duration, a reverberation label, pathological fixation, and feedforward persistence. The viability treatment likewise prevents a preserved signal or organism-level function from automatically licensing the target referent.

Appendix F now identifies two instructor-level refinements rather than presenting them as quotations from the papers:

- the interval decision convention used to teach uncertainty conservatively; and
- the strict ablation diagnostic rule used to prevent a point estimate from standing in for complete retained-component evidence.

The bearer-complete synthesis is also labeled as a teaching convention and proposed corpus-level reconciliation, not as wording already adopted by every paper.

### E. Ablation, differentiation, and validation

The audit removed a recurring risk of treating profile disagreement as bearer disagreement. The manual now separates:

- logical inclusion in a conjunction;
- profile-level diagnostic disagreement;
- bearer-output disagreement after all rivals are propagated through the frozen candidate lattice;
- incremental prediction against a declared rival and independent anchor family; and
- selective causal contribution under intervention.

Criterion, mechanism, measurement, grouped, profile, and bearer audits remain distinct. A licensed intervention must move the target by its preregistered minimum, preserve retained components within complete equivalence intervals, preserve viability and referent identity, address alternate routes, and recompute the bearer output.

Validation is presented as a sequence of risky tests: synthetic construct recovery, diagnostic near-miss coverage, selective perturbation and rescue, held-out transport, versioned rival prediction, independent implementation and readout, and prospective outcome-to-revision commitments. Success at one stage cannot be promoted to metaphysical identity or final confirmation.

### F. Phenomenal character and schematic legibility

Phenomenal character is gated behind, but not reduced to, a positive presence classification. Its relational bridge is tested independently; a failed bridge does not negate presence or license denial of all character. Surviving automorphisms are reported as underdetermination.

Schematic legibility remains modular. Decomposable semantics, compositional readout, counterfactual fault localization, evidential independence, teachability, and transport are assessed at the proper trial or dataset unit. Blind-reader agreement supports public reconstructability under frozen rules, not the truth of the theory.

### H. Collaborative learning, worksheets, and assessment

Every session includes collaborative application and discussion, not only lecture exposition. Formats include jigsaws, structured academic controversy, reciprocal hearings, design studios, progressive-disclosure conferences, evidence tribunals, blind readout, transport audits, skeptical panels, and outcome-to-revision tribunals. Private commitment, rotating roles, minority notes, and concrete group artifacts reduce dominance and hindsight effects.

Worksheets 1-9 now occupy dedicated instructor-manual pages 115-123. Each begins with an explanation of its purpose, appropriate use, and inferential limits. The audit made two forms genuinely writable:

- Worksheet 3 now includes a blank team-record column for each boundary decision; and
- Worksheet 6 now includes a blank decision-and-evidence column for each legibility criterion.

Session 11 now uses Worksheet 8 for progressive evidence rounds and Worksheet 1 for the final frozen classification, removing the previous workflow conflict. Assessment rewards target accuracy, inferential license, rival quality, sensitivity to missing evidence, principled revision, and documented dissent rather than agreement with the theory.

The manual avoids color-only encoding and supports linearized figures, editable tables, read-aloud equations, written or asynchronous participation, quiet preparation, nonverbal response, remote participation, and flexible presentation roles.

The audience parser is now fail closed against an exact, ordered inventory of 106 blocks: 1 shared, 39 student, 50 reveal, and 16 instructor blocks. It rejects unclassified substantive content, malformed or nested markers, duplicate IDs, missing required blocks, leakage, additions, removals, reordering, or audience reassignment.

The release contains:

Each is independently converted and verified for its intended heading, absence of other reveals and keys, one-page limit, one outline entry, embedded fonts, PDF tags, `en-US` language, and descriptive metadata. ## 5. Residual limitations

These are limitations of warrant or future external validation, not known release defects:

1. **The theory remains empirically open.** The manual specifies what would count for and against the model but does not claim those studies have already succeeded.
2. **The bearer-complete synthesis should be versioned in the research corpus.** The manual labels the refinement accurately; a later theory note should accept, revise, or reject it explicitly.
3. **Independent anchors remain fallible and partly theory-laden.** Real results must preserve anchor genealogy, disagreement, held-out adjudication, and rival-relative scope.
4. **Threshold and transport robustness remains empirical.** Real studies need preregistered calibration, sensitivity neighborhoods, domain-shift tests, and ranking-reversal reporting.
5. **Synthetic classroom values are not evidence about actual systems.** They must not be reused for clinical, welfare, or artificial-consciousness judgments.
6. **Specialist accessibility testing remains desirable.** Structural PDF checks passed, but assistive-technology users should test representative prose, equations, tables, figures, forms, and reveals.
7. **Pedagogical outcome validity remains unmeasured.** A pilot should collect completion times, error patterns, transfer performance, rubric agreement, and accessibility feedback.
8. **External references can drift.** Bibliographic metadata, URLs, and rival-theory version cards should be rechecked when the corpus baseline changes.
9. **Multi-file publication is not power-loss atomic.** The build lock prevents concurrent writers and rollback handles ordinary in-process failures, but an operating-system or power failure during sequential replacement could still require a clean rebuild.
10. **A standalone worksheet pack is an optional convenience enhancement.** The requested forms are already on dedicated instructor-manual pages; a separate key-free packet would require an explicit distribution decision.

## 6. Release verdict

**Approved for controlled graduate-level teaching distribution.**

No known blocking weakness remains in the audited source or generated teaching package. Distribution must preserve the audience policy: release the student base pack at course start, release only the assigned single-sheet reveal at each commitment point, and retain the reveal master and instructor manual under instructor control.

This verdict concerns coherence, pedagogy, packaging, and artifact integrity. It is not a verdict that Cø / N\* is true, that the conjunction has been empirically validated, or that any target system is conscious.
